Welcome to the Official website of the FCI Obedience World Championship 2024

The FCI Obedience World Championship will take place from 25 to 28 July near the Riga city at the Horse Riding Centre Lielceri.

There will be also a Facebook support group which will be offer later and where You will find fresh information all the time.

The Latvian Cynological Federation (L.K.F.) is proud and excited to organize this great event of dogs sports and looks forward to meet all participants in July 2024. Be so kind and visit this site regularly to find step by step more information.
