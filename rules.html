Regulations

  • FCI Obedience Regulations

Veterinary inspection

  1. Please note that the dogs participating in the FCI Obedience World Championship might be required to undertake a veterinary inspection during the event.
  2. These veterinary inspections may be fixed or itinerant and shall be carried out by certified veterinary doctors. Before the start of competition, the responsible veterinarian is authorized to check the corresponding health documentation of the dogs, also at any time during the event. The responsible veterinarian could check any illness upon a request. The veterinary doctor may decide not allow a dog to participate in the Championship if veterinary documents are not in order or a dog shows clinical signs of illness
  3. In accordance by The Latvian laws and regulations bitches in heat cannot participate in any competition.
  4. Veterinarians are on hand to assist participants during the event.

Cropped ears and docked tails

  • In Latvian Republic dogs with cropped ears and docked tails can participate in competition if in their country of residence this operations are allowed.

Animal welfare

  • Latvian Cynological Federation always take care for animal welfare, supports animal welfare societies and our priority is welfare of dogs.
